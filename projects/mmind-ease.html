LLaMA-based Mental Health Assistant

This project focuses on fine-tuning a LLaMA-based large language model using QLoRA to provide culturally-aware mental health support. The system is designed for anxiety and depression-related conversations while maintaining privacy and ethical AI principles.

Results

The model demonstrated improved contextual understanding and culturally relevant responses compared to base models.
